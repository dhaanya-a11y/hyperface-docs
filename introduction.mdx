# Introduction

Welcome to the **Offer Management** documentation.

The Offer Management module enables issuers to create, configure, publish, and manage customer reward campaigns through an intuitive step-by-step workflow. Depending on the business requirement, different offer types can be configured to reward customers for eligible transactions, spending milestones, merchant purchases, and other campaign objectives.

This documentation provides detailed guidance for configuring each offer type, including eligibility criteria, reward calculation, budgeting, display settings, communication, and publishing.

---

## Offer Types

Hyperface supports four offer types, each suited to a different reward strategy. Select the type that best matches your campaign objective, then follow its dedicated guide for full configuration instructions.

### Transactional Offer

A Transactional Offer rewards customers when an eligible transaction satisfies the configured conditions. This offer type is suitable for campaigns that reward individual transactions based on predefined eligibility criteria.

Typical use cases include fuel cashback, shopping rewards, dining offers, travel promotions, and utility bill payment campaigns.

### Milestone Offer

A Milestone Offer rewards customers after they achieve predefined spending or transaction milestones within a specified period. Rewards are issued only after the configured milestone conditions have been successfully completed.

Typical use cases include monthly spending campaigns, transaction count campaigns, and customer engagement programs.

### Merchant Offer

A Merchant Offer rewards customers for making eligible purchases at participating merchants. Eligibility is determined using merchant-specific criteria, making it ideal for targeted merchant promotions and partner campaigns.

Typical use cases include restaurant cashback, shopping promotions, travel partnerships, and brand collaboration campaigns.

### Gamified Offer

A Gamified Offer rewards customers through interactive campaigns and engagement-based activities. Customers complete predefined challenges or objectives to unlock rewards, creating a more engaging reward experience.

Typical use cases include spin-the-wheel campaigns, scratch card promotions, seasonal challenges, and customer engagement campaigns.

## Documentation Structure

Each offer guide follows a consistent step-by-step configuration journey, covering:

- Creating a new offer
- Configuring offer details
- Setting up the offer user base
- Defining eligibility conditions
- Configuring reward calculation
- Setting budgets and approvals
- Configuring display elements
- Setting up communication
- Reviewing and publishing the offer

---

## Before You Begin

Before creating an offer, ensure that:

- The required issuer and program are available.
- The appropriate reward outcome has been identified.
- Any required customer eligibility criteria are defined.
- The necessary permissions are available to create and publish offers.

<Note>

The available configuration steps may vary depending on the selected offer type.

</Note>
