# Offer Types

Hyperface supports multiple Offer Types, each designed for a specific business objective. Select an Offer Type based on how customers should become eligible for rewards.

## Transactional Offer

A **Transactional Offer** rewards customers when an eligible transaction satisfies the configured conditions. This Offer Type is suitable for campaigns that reward individual transactions based on predefined eligibility criteria.

Typical use cases include fuel cashback, shopping rewards, dining offers, travel promotions, and utility bill payment campaigns.

![Transactional Offer](/images/c.png)

➡️ **Learn more:** [Transactional Offer](/transaction-offer/introduction)

---

## Milestone Offer

A **Milestone Offer** rewards customers after they achieve predefined spending or transaction milestones within a specified period. Rewards are issued only after the configured milestone conditions have been successfully completed.

Typical use cases include monthly spending campaigns, transaction count campaigns, and customer engagement programs.

![Milestone Offer](/images/0.2.png)

➡️ **Learn more:** [Milestone Offer](/milestone-offer)

---

## Merchant Offer

A **Merchant Offer** rewards customers for making eligible purchases at participating merchants. Eligibility is determined using merchant-specific criteria, making it ideal for targeted merchant promotions and partner campaigns.

Typical use cases include restaurant cashback, shopping promotions, travel partnerships, and brand collaboration campaigns.

![Merchant Offer](/images/1.png)

➡️ **Learn more:** [Merchant Offer](/merchant-offer)

---

## Gamified Offer

A **Gamified Offer** rewards customers through interactive campaigns and engagement-based activities. Customers complete predefined challenges or objectives to unlock rewards, creating a more engaging reward experience.

Typical use cases include spin-the-wheel campaigns, scratch card promotions, seasonal challenges, and customer engagement campaigns.

![Gamified Offer](/images/0.png)

➡️ **Learn more:** [Gamified Offer](/gamified-offer)
