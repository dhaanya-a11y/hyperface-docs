title: Budget
description: Configure the budget allocated for the offer and define the spending limits for reward distribution.
---

The **Budget** section defines the total reward allocation available for an offer. It helps issuers control campaign spending by specifying the maximum budget that can be utilized throughout the offer lifecycle.

In addition to budget allocation, this step also captures the approver responsible for reviewing and approving the configured budget before the offer can be published.

![Step 8 - Configure Budget](/images/j.png)

## Budget Configuration

Configure the following fields before proceeding:

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Set Budget** | Specifies the total reward budget allocated for the offer. Rewards will be issued only until the configured budget is exhausted. | ₹10,00,000 |
| **Approver** | Select the user responsible for reviewing and approving the configured budget before the offer is published. | John Doe |

<Info>
An offer cannot be published until the configured budget has been reviewed and approved by the selected approver.
</Info>

---

## Advanced Settings

Expand **Advanced Settings** to configure additional budget-related parameters, such as budget controls and advanced allocation settings, based on your campaign requirements.

---

## Complete the Configuration

After completing the Budget configuration:

1. Verify the configured budget amount.
2. Confirm the selected approver.
3. Review any advanced settings, if configured.
4. Click **Continue** to proceed to the **Usage Limits** configuration.
