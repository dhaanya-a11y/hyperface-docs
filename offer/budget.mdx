## Overview

The **Budget** section enables issuers to control the total reward allocation for an offer. By defining a budget, you can manage campaign spending, monitor reward distribution, and ensure rewards are issued only within the configured limits.

Configure the appropriate budget before publishing the offer to maintain effective control over campaign expenditure.

<Note>

The configured budget represents the maximum reward amount that can be distributed for the offer. Once the available budget is exhausted, no additional rewards are issued until the budget is increased or the offer is updated.

</Note>

---

## Budget Configuration

Configure the following fields before proceeding.

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Set Budget** | Specifies the total reward budget allocated for the offer. Rewards are distributed only while sufficient budget remains available. | ₹10,00,000 |
| **Approver** | Select the user responsible for reviewing and approving the configured budget before the offer is published. | John Doe |

### Set Budget

The configured budget acts as the maximum reward allocation for the campaign. Every reward issued as part of the offer is deducted from this budget until it is exhausted.

**Example**

If the configured budget is **₹10,00,000**, rewards continue to be distributed until the total rewards issued reach **₹10,00,000**.

### Approver

Select the user responsible for reviewing and approving the configured budget.

The offer cannot be published until the selected approver reviews and approves the budget configuration.

<Warning>

Budget approval is mandatory before an offer can be published. Ensure the correct approver is selected before proceeding.

</Warning>

---

## Advanced Settings

Expand **Advanced Settings** to configure additional budget controls and allocation settings based on your campaign requirements.

<Tip>

Use **Advanced Settings** only when additional budget controls are required for your campaign. For standard campaigns, the default budget configuration is generally sufficient.

</Tip>

---

## What Happens After Configuration?

Once the budget is approved, the configured amount becomes the maximum reward allocation available for the offer.

As customers earn rewards, the corresponding reward value is deducted from the available budget. Rewards continue to be distributed until the configured budget is exhausted.

---

## Complete the Configuration

After configuring the **Budget**:

1. Review the configured **Budget** amount.
2. Confirm the selected **Approver**.
3. Review any **Advanced Settings**, if configured.
4. Click **Continue** to proceed to the **Usage Limits** configuration.
