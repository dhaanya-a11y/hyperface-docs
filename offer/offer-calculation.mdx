## What is Offer Calculation?

Offer Calculation defines how the reward value is derived once a transaction, milestone, or level qualifies — for example, as a flat amount, a percentage of spend, or a slab-based structure.

## Why is it used?

It exists to determine the actual monetary or points value a customer receives, which directly shapes both the customer's incentive and the campaign's cost.
