Defines how customers become eligible to receive a reward, and is the first choice made in the offer creation journey. Each Offer Type supports a specific business objective and determines the eligibility model — a single transaction, accumulated spend, merchant-specific activity, or engagement-based activity — under which rewards are issued.

Hyperface supports four Offer Types: **Transactional**, **Milestone**, **Merchant**, and **Gamified**.

<Warning>
Offer Type determines which configuration screens and options appear for the rest of the offer creation journey. Confirm the correct Offer Type before proceeding — switching it after later steps have been configured may require reconfiguring those steps.
</Warning>

## Comparing Offer Types

| Consideration | Transactional | Milestone | Merchant | Gamified |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Reward triggered by | A single qualifying transaction | Reaching a predefined spend or transaction milestone within a period | An eligible purchase at a participating merchant | Completing an interactive challenge or level |
| Evaluation | Each transaction evaluated independently | Progress accumulated until the milestone is achieved | Eligibility based on merchant-specific criteria | Progress tracked through configured levels or activities |
| Best for | Straightforward cashback or reward campaigns | Encouraging sustained spend or engagement over time | Merchant-funded or partner-specific promotions | Engagement-driven, interactive campaigns |

### How Offer Type shapes the rest of the journey

The eligibility step shown later in the journey depends on the Offer Type selected here:

| Offer Type | Eligibility step shown later |
|---|---|
| Transactional | **Transaction Eligibility** — conditions based on individual transaction attributes |
| Milestone | **Milestone Eligibility** — progress tracked towards a spend or transaction threshold |
| Gamified | **Gamification Levels** — progress tracked through configured levels or challenges |
| Merchant | No separate eligibility screen — eligibility is defined through merchant selection as part of **Offer Details** and **Offer User Base** |

## Transactional Offer
A **Transactional Offer** rewards customers when an individual transaction satisfies the configured eligibility criteria. Each qualifying transaction is evaluated independently, making this Offer Type suitable for cashback and transaction-based reward campaigns.
![Step 2 - Select the Offer Type](/images/c.png)

---
## Milestone Offer
A **Milestone Offer** rewards customers after successfully achieving predefined spending or transaction milestones within a specified period. Rewards are issued only after all configured milestone conditions are met.

![Step 1 - Select Offer Type](/images/0.2.png)
---
## Merchant Offer
A **Merchant Offer** rewards customers for making eligible purchases at participating merchants. Eligibility is determined using merchant-specific criteria, making it suitable for merchant-funded and partner-specific campaigns.
![Step 1 - Create a Merchant Offer](/images/1.png) 

---
## Gamified Offer
A **Gamified Offer** rewards customers through interactive and engagement-based campaigns. Customers participate in activities or challenges to unlock rewards, creating an engaging reward experience.

## Example: Choosing the correct Offer Type

A bank wants to reward customers who spend a total of ₹50,000 across transactions in a calendar month, rather than rewarding any single transaction.

1. Select **Milestone Offer**, since the reward depends on cumulative spend rather than one qualifying transaction.
2. Continue with the remaining setup.

Selecting **Transactional Offer** here would be incorrect — it would reward every qualifying transaction individually instead of tracking progress towards the ₹50,000 target.

## Which Offer Type Should You Select?

<Tip>
- Reward every qualifying transaction on its own → select **Transactional Offer**.
- Reward customers only after they reach a cumulative spend or transaction target → select **Milestone Offer**.
- Reward purchases made specifically at participating merchants → select **Merchant Offer**.
- Reward customers for completing interactive challenges or activities → select **Gamified Offer**.
</Tip>
