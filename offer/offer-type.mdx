# Offer Type 

## What is an Offer Type?

An **Offer Type** defines the business objective of an Offer and determines how customers become eligible to receive a reward. It serves as the foundation of every Offer by defining the qualification logic, reward behaviour, and the configuration journey that follows.

Hyperface supports multiple Offer Types to address different campaign objectives. Depending on the business requirement, an Offer can reward customers for individual transactions, milestone achievements, merchant-specific purchases, or interactive engagement campaigns.

The following screen displays the available Offer Types that can be selected while creating an Offer.

---

## Transactional Offer

A **Transactional Offer** rewards customers when an eligible transaction satisfies the configured conditions. Every qualifying transaction is evaluated independently, allowing rewards to be issued immediately after the transaction meets the defined eligibility criteria.

This Offer Type is suitable for campaigns that encourage customers to increase spending or perform specific types of transactions.

**Typical Business Use Cases**

- Fuel Cashback
- Shopping Rewards
- Dining Offers
- UPI Cashback Campaigns
- Bill Payment Offers

## Milestone Offer

A **Milestone Offer** rewards customers after they achieve predefined spending or transaction milestones within a specified period. Instead of rewarding every eligible transaction, the reward is issued only after the configured milestone conditions are successfully completed.

This Offer Type is commonly used to improve customer engagement and encourage higher spending over time.

**Typical Business Use Cases**

- Spend ₹50,000 and earn Cashback
- Complete 10 transactions and earn Reward Points
- Monthly Spending Campaigns
- Customer Retention Programs

![Milestone Offer](/images/0.2.png)

➡️ **Learn more:** [Milestone Offer Configuration](/milestone-offer/introduction)

## Merchant Offer

A **Merchant Offer** rewards customers for making eligible purchases at participating merchants. Eligibility is determined using merchant-specific criteria, allowing issuers to create targeted promotional campaigns with partner merchants.

This Offer Type is ideal for increasing customer engagement with selected merchants and driving merchant-specific transactions.

**Typical Business Use Cases**

- Restaurant Cashback
- Shopping Promotions
- Travel Partner Offers
- Brand Collaboration Campaigns

![Merchant Offer](/images/1.png)

➡️ **Learn more:** [Merchant Offer Configuration](/merchant-offer/introduction)

## Gamified Offer

A **Gamified Offer** rewards customers through interactive campaigns and engagement-driven experiences. Instead of rewarding customers for a single transaction, this Offer Type encourages participation through challenges, activities, or predefined objectives. Rewards are unlocked as customers complete the configured goals, making campaigns more engaging and interactive.

Gamified Offers are commonly used to improve customer participation, increase engagement, and create memorable reward experiences through game-like mechanics.

**Typical Business Use Cases**

- Spin the Wheel campaigns
- Scratch Card promotions
- Seasonal engagement campaigns
- Customer participation programs
- Interactive loyalty campaigns
