## What is Offer Type?

Offer Type is the choice that determines which reward strategy a campaign follows. Hyperface supports four Offer Types, each suited to a different way of qualifying customers for a reward.

## Why is it used?

Selecting the right Offer Type up front determines which eligibility, calculation, and reward options are available later in the configuration journey, so it is the first decision made when creating any offer.

[Placeholder: Offer Type selection screenshot]

### Transactional Offer

Rewards a customer for a single qualifying transaction, evaluated independently against the configured eligibility criteria. Best suited for instant, one-time rewards tied to individual purchases.

### Milestone Offer

Rewards a customer once they reach a predefined spending or transaction milestone within the offer period, based on cumulative activity rather than a single transaction.

[Placeholder: Milestone Offer screenshot]

[View Milestone Offer documentation](/milestone-offer/introduction)

### Merchant Offer

Rewards a customer for making qualifying purchases at specific participating merchants, with eligibility determined by merchant-specific criteria.

[Placeholder: Merchant Offer screenshot]

[View Merchant Offer documentation](/merchant-offer/introduction)

### Gamified Offer

Rewards a customer progressively as they cross multiple spending or transaction-count thresholds — or levels — within a defined period, unlocking a distinct reward at each level.
