# Offer Type

## What is an Offer Type?

An Offer Type defines how customers become eligible to receive rewards. Hyperface supports multiple Offer Types, each designed to achieve a specific business objective.

---

## Transactional Offer

A **Transactional Offer** rewards customers when an eligible transaction satisfies the configured eligibility criteria. Each qualifying transaction is evaluated independently, making it suitable for transaction-based reward campaigns such as cashback, shopping, dining, fuel, travel, and utility bill payments.

## Milestone Offer

A **Milestone Offer** rewards customers after they successfully achieve predefined spending or transaction milestones within a specified period. Rewards are issued only after the configured milestone conditions have been completed.

## Merchant Offer

A **Merchant Offer** rewards customers for making eligible purchases at participating merchants. Eligibility is determined using merchant-specific conditions, making it suitable for targeted merchant promotions and partner campaigns.

## Gamified Offer

A **Gamified Offer** rewards customers through interactive campaigns and engagement-based activities. Customers complete challenges or activities to unlock rewards, creating a more engaging reward experience.
