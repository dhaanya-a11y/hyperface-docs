# Offer Type

## What is an Offer Type?

An Offer Type defines how customers become eligible to receive a reward. Each Offer Type is designed to support a specific campaign objective and determines the eligibility criteria, reward logic, and overall customer experience.

Hyperface supports multiple Offer Types to help issuers create reward campaigns for different business use cases.

![Offer Type](/images/offer-type.png)

---

## Transactional Offer

A **Transactional Offer** rewards customers when an individual transaction satisfies the configured eligibility criteria. Each qualifying transaction is evaluated independently, making this Offer Type suitable for transaction-based reward campaigns.

Typical use cases include cashback on fuel, dining, shopping, travel, utility bill payments, and other eligible purchase transactions.

## Milestone Offer

A **Milestone Offer** rewards customers after they successfully achieve predefined spending or transaction milestones within a specified period. Rewards are issued only after the configured milestone conditions have been completed.

Typical use cases include monthly spend campaigns, transaction count–based rewards, customer engagement programs, and loyalty milestone campaigns.

## Merchant Offer

A **Merchant Offer** rewards customers for making eligible purchases at participating merchants. Eligibility is determined using merchant-specific conditions such as participating merchants or merchant categories, making it suitable for targeted merchant campaigns.

Typical use cases include restaurant offers, retail shopping promotions, travel partner offers, and brand-specific reward campaigns.

## Gamified Offer

A **Gamified Offer** rewards customers through interactive and engagement-based campaigns. Customers participate in activities or challenges to unlock rewards, creating a more engaging reward experience.

Typical use cases include spin-the-wheel campaigns, scratch card promotions, festive campaigns, and customer engagement initiatives.
