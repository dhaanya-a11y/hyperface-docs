# Offer Type

## What is an Offer Type?

An Offer Type defines how customers become eligible to receive a reward. Each Offer Type supports a specific business objective and determines the eligibility conditions under which rewards are issued.

Hyperface supports four Offer Types: Transactional, Milestone, Merchant, and Gamified.

---

## Transactional Offer

A **Transactional Offer** rewards customers when an individual transaction satisfies the configured eligibility criteria. Each qualifying transaction is evaluated independently, making this Offer Type suitable for cashback and transaction-based reward campaigns.

## Milestone Offer

A **Milestone Offer** rewards customers after successfully achieving predefined spending or transaction milestones within a specified period. Rewards are issued only after all configured milestone conditions are met.

## Merchant Offer

A **Merchant Offer** rewards customers for making eligible purchases at participating merchants. Eligibility is determined using merchant-specific criteria, making it suitable for merchant-funded and partner-specific campaigns.

A **Gamified Offer** rewards customers through interactive and engagement-based campaigns. Customers participate in activities or challenges to unlock rewards, creating an engaging reward experience.
