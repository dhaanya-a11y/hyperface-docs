The **Posting** section defines **when** the configured offer benefit is credited to the customer's account and **under what conditions** the posting should occur.
After configuring **Usage Limits**, use this step to configure the posting schedule, define posting eligibility conditions, and specify the ledger narrations that should be recorded when benefits are posted or reversed.

/>

<Note>
**Posting** controls only the reward crediting process. A customer must first satisfy all previously configured offer conditions before the configured posting rules are evaluated.
</Note>

---

## Enable Posting

Enable **Posting** to allow the configured offer benefit to be credited to the customer's account.

If Posting is disabled, the offer benefit is not posted even when all eligibility conditions are satisfied.

---

## Configure Posting Eligibility
Configure the conditions that must be satisfied before the reward is posted.
For each posting eligibility condition:
- Select the required eligibility attribute.
- Configure the corresponding value.
- Specify whether the condition is **Allowed** or **Restricted**, as applicable.
- Click **Add More** to configure additional posting eligibility conditions.

<Tip>

Use multiple posting eligibility conditions when the reward should be posted only for specific customer segments, products, or transaction scenarios.

</Tip>

---

## Configure Offer Posting

Choose when the configured benefit should be credited.

| Posting Option | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Post Immediately** | Credits the benefit immediately after all configured posting conditions are satisfied. |
| **Schedule** | Credits the benefit on the configured date and time. |

### Post Immediately
**When to use it**
Choose **Post Immediately** when customers should receive their reward as soon as all configured conditions are satisfied.
**Example**
Reward customers with cashback immediately after an eligible purchase is successfully processed.

### Schedule
**When to use it**
Choose **Schedule** when rewards should be credited at a later date or during a predefined posting window.
**Example**
Credit monthly cashback to all eligible customers on the **1st day of the following month**.

<Warning>
Changing the posting schedule after an offer is active may affect when future rewards are credited. Review the configured posting option before publishing the offer.
</Warning>

---

## Configure Narration
Provide the ledger narration that should be recorded during posting and reversal.
<img
  src="/images/l.1.png"
  alt="Step 10 - Configure Narration"
  style={{ height: "300px", width: "400px" }}
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

Configure the following fields:
| Field | Description | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Posting Narration** | Message recorded in the ledger when the reward is successfully posted. | Cashback credited for Fuel Offer |
| **Reversal Narration** | Message recorded in the ledger if the posted reward is reversed. | Cashback reversed due to transaction reversal |

<Note>
Posting and Reversal Narrations are recorded for operational and audit purposes. Choose clear and meaningful descriptions so posted and reversed rewards can be easily identified.
</Note>

---

## What Happens After Configuration?
Once the configured posting conditions are satisfied, the reward is credited according to the selected **Posting Option**.
If **Allow Reversal** was enabled during **Offer Calculation**, the configured **Reversal Narration** is used whenever a posted reward is subsequently reversed.

---

## Complete the Configuration
After completing the **Posting** configuration:
1. Verify that **Posting** is enabled, if required.
2. Review the configured **Posting Eligibility** conditions.
3. Confirm the selected **Posting Option**.
4. Review the configured **Posting Narration** and **Reversal Narration**.
5. Click **Continue** to proceed to the **Display Elements** configuration.
