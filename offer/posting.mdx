## What is Posting?

Posting determines when and how a qualified reward is credited to a customer's account, including the timing of the credit and the narration recorded against it.

## Why is it used?

It exists so that issuers can control the customer-facing timing of a reward — for example, delaying it to allow for transaction reversals or refunds before the reward is finalized.
