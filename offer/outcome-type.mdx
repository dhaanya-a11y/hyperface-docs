# Outcome Type

## What is an Outcome Type?

An Outcome Type defines the reward or benefit that a customer receives after successfully meeting an offer's eligibility criteria. It determines the type of benefit that will be issued once the offer conditions are fulfilled.

During offer creation, select the Outcome Type that best aligns with your campaign objective.

![Step 3 - Select the Outcome Type](/images/d.png)

---

## Cashback

Cashback credits a predefined monetary amount or percentage of the eligible transaction value to the customer's account after the offer conditions are successfully met.

---

## Reward Points

Reward Points award loyalty points to customers upon successful completion of the offer conditions. The credited points can be accumulated and redeemed as part of the issuer's rewards program.

---

## Voucher

Voucher issues a digital or physical voucher to eligible customers after the configured offer conditions are satisfied. The voucher can then be redeemed according to the campaign rules.

---

## Smart Tag

Smart Tag assigns a predefined tag to customers after they qualify for an offer. These tags can be used for customer segmentation and to enable targeted campaigns in the future.

---

## Accelerated Reward

Accelerated Reward increases the reward earning rate for eligible transactions, allowing customers to earn enhanced rewards compared to the standard reward program.

---

## Partner Points

Partner Points credit loyalty points from a participating partner rewards program instead of the issuer's standard reward points, based on the configured campaign.
