## What is Outcome Type?

Outcome Type is the choice that determines the reward a customer receives once they qualify for an offer.

## Why is it used?

The selected Outcome Type shapes the customer's experience of the reward — whether it is a direct monetary credit, a points balance, a redeemable coupon, or something else — so it is chosen based on the campaign's objective.

[Placeholder: Outcome Type selection screenshot]

### Cashback

A monetary reward credited back to the customer's account once a qualifying transaction, milestone, or level is completed.

[Placeholder: Cashback screenshot]

### Reward Points

A points-based currency credited to the customer, redeemable later according to the program's reward policy.

[Placeholder: Reward Points screenshot]

### Voucher

A coupon-style reward the customer can redeem for a specific benefit, such as a discount or a product or service.

[Placeholder: Voucher screenshot]

### Smart Tag

A non-monetary outcome that attaches a tag to the customer's profile for segmentation, targeting, or eligibility in future campaigns.

[Placeholder: Smart Tag screenshot]

### Accelerated Reward

An outcome that boosts the rate at which a customer earns rewards, rather than issuing a separate, fixed reward.

[Placeholder: Accelerated Reward screenshot]

### Partner Points

A points-based reward issued in a third-party partner's loyalty currency, rather than the issuer's own rewards program.
