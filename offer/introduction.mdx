Offer Management enables issuers to create, configure, and manage customer reward campaigns in the Hyperface Dashboard.

Every offer follows a common configuration journey, regardless of its type. During offer creation, issuers configure common concepts such as **Offer Type**, **Outcome Type**, **Offer Scope**, **Offer User Base**, **Eligibility**, **Offer Calculation**, **Budget**, **Usage Limits**, **Posting**, **Display Elements**, and **Communication**.

This section introduces these common concepts and provides an overview of each configuration step before moving on to the detailed configuration of individual offer types.

During the offer creation process, issuers configure the following common components:

- **Offer Type** – Defines the type of reward campaign being created.
- **Outcome Type** – Specifies the reward customers receive after meeting the offer conditions.
- **Offer Scope** – Determines where the offer is applicable, including the issuer and program.
- **Offer User Base** – Defines the customers eligible to participate in the offer.
- **Eligibility** – Configures the rules and conditions customers must satisfy.
- **Offer Calculation** – Defines how rewards are calculated and awarded.
- **Budget** – Allocates and manages the campaign budget.
- **Usage Limits** – Controls how frequently customers can receive rewards.
- **Posting** – Configures when and how rewards are credited.
- **Display Elements** – Defines how offers are presented within the customer experience.
- **Communication** – Configures customer-facing notifications and messaging.

Each of these configuration steps is explained in detail throughout this documentation, providing the information required to successfully create, configure, and publish offers using the Hyperface Dashboard.
