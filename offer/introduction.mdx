# Introduction

Offer Management enables issuers to create, configure, and manage customer reward campaigns in the Hyperface Dashboard.

Every offer follows a common configuration journey, regardless of its type. During offer creation, issuers configure common concepts such as Offer Type, Outcome Type, Offer Scope, Offer User Base, Eligibility, Offer Calculation, Budget, Usage Limits, Posting, Display Elements, and Communication.

This section explains these common concepts before moving on to the detailed configuration of individual Offer Types.
