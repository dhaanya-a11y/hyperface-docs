Offer Management enables issuers to create, configure, and manage customer reward campaigns through the Hyperface Dashboard.

Whether you're creating a **Transactional Offer** or a **Milestone Offer**, every offer follows a common configuration journey. Each step in the workflow captures a specific aspect of the offer, ensuring all required configurations are completed before the offer is published.

During the offer creation process, issuers configure the following components:

- **Offer Type** – Select the type of offer to be created.
- **Outcome Type** – Define the reward that customers receive upon meeting the offer criteria.
- **Offer Scope** – Specify the issuer and program where the offer will be applicable.
- **Offer User Base** – Identify the customers eligible for the offer.
- **Eligibility** – Configure the conditions that transactions or milestones must satisfy.
- **Offer Calculation** – Define how the reward is calculated.
- **Budget** – Configure the budget allocated for the offer.
- **Usage Limits** – Define how frequently customers can receive the reward.
- **Posting** – Configure when and how rewards are credited.
- **Display Elements** – Define the customer-facing content displayed for the offer.
- **Communication** – Configure the notifications associated with the offer.
