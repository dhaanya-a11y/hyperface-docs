## What is Offer Management?

Offer Management is the part of the Hyperface Dashboard where issuers create, configure, and publish customer reward campaigns.

## Why is it used?

This section introduces the concepts that are common across every Offer Type — such as how outcomes are defined, how eligibility is scoped, how budgets and usage limits work, and how offers are posted, displayed, and communicated to customers. Each Offer Type's own documentation then shows how these shared concepts are configured step by step for that specific offer.
