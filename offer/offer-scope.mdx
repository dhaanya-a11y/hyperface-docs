## What is Offer Scope?

Offer Scope defines the boundaries within which an offer is applicable — including the issuer, the program type, and the specific programs the offer is associated with.

## Why is it used?

It exists so that an offer only becomes active for the card programs and customer base it was actually designed for, rather than being applied broadly across unrelated programs by default.
