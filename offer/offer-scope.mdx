## Overview

Hyperface enables issuers to create offers that can be tailored to different issuers, program types, and programs within the organization. **Offer Scope** defines where an offer is applicable, ensuring it is available only within the intended business context.

During this step, configure the **Issuer**, **Program Type**, and **Programs** for which the offer should be available. The selected scope establishes the offer's applicability before proceeding to configure the **Offer User Base**.

<Note>

**Offer Scope** defines **where** an offer is applicable. It does **not** determine **who** is eligible to receive the offer. Customer eligibility is configured separately in **Offer User Base**.

</Note>

---

## Basic Offer Details

Provide the core details that identify the offer and define when it runs.

| Field | What It Represents | Example |
|-------|---------------------|---------|
| **Offer Name** | A unique, identifiable name for the offer in the Hyperface Dashboard. | Fuel Cashback July 2026 |
| **Offer Description** | A brief description of the offer for internal reference in the dashboard. | 5% cashback on eligible fuel transactions |
| **Offer Duration** | The start and end date during which the offer remains active. Transactions outside this window are not evaluated for the offer. | 01 Jul 2026, 00:00 – 31 Jul 2026, 23:59 |

### Eligibility Level

**Eligibility Level** determines whether offer eligibility is evaluated per account or across all accounts belonging to a customer.

| Consideration | Account Level | Customer Level |
|---|---|---|
| Eligibility evaluated for | Each account independently | All accounts belonging to the customer |
| Best for | Offers tied to a specific account or card | Offers meant to reward the overall customer relationship, regardless of which account is used |

<Tip>
Changing the **Eligibility Level** changes what is considered a single eligible unit.

- Choose **Account Level** when the offer should apply separately to each account a customer holds.
- Choose **Customer Level** when activity across all customer accounts should count towards the same offer.
</Tip>

**Example: Rewarding a customer's overall relationship**

To run a cashback offer that rewards customers regardless of which of their accounts is used:

1. Set **Eligibility Level** to **Customer Level**.
2. Continue with the remaining setup.

Transactions made from any account belonging to the customer are considered together for this offer.

<Tip>

**Not sure which to pick?**

- Choose **Account Level** if the offer is tied to a specific product, such as a particular card.
- Choose **Customer Level** if the offer should reward the customer's overall relationship, regardless of which account or product is used.

</Tip>

## Offer Applicability

Define where the offer is available.

| Field | What It Represents | Example |
|-------|---------------------|---------|
| **Issuer** | The issuer for which the offer will be available. | HDFC Bank |
| **Program Type** | The type of program where the offer will be applicable. | Credit Card |
| **Apply to all current and future programs** | When enabled, the offer applies to every existing program of the selected type and automatically applies to any new program created later. | Enabled |
| **Program** | The specific program(s) to which the offer applies. This field is displayed only when **Apply to all current and future programs** is disabled. | Platinum Credit Card |

<Note>
The **Program** field is required only when **Apply to all current and future programs** is disabled. If enabled, the offer automatically applies to all existing and future programs of the selected **Program Type**.
</Note>

<Warning>
Changing the configured **Issuer**, **Program Type**, or **Program** after completing later configuration steps may require those configurations to be reviewed or updated.
</Warning>

**Example: Restricting an offer to one program**

To launch a cashback offer only for the **Platinum Credit Card** program:

1. Select the applicable **Issuer** and **Program Type**.
2. Leave **Apply to all current and future programs** disabled.
3. Select **Platinum Credit Card** under **Program**.

Only transactions made under the **Platinum Credit Card** program are considered for this offer, even if the customer holds other programs under the same issuer.

---

## What Happens After Configuration?

Offer Scope establishes the offer's identity, eligibility level, and the issuer and programs to which it applies. Once configured, these details define the boundaries within which the **Offer User Base** and **Transaction Eligibility** are evaluated.

A transaction is considered for the offer only if it falls within the configured **Offer Duration** and belongs to an applicable **Issuer** and **Program**.

---

## Complete the Configuration

After configuring the **Offer Scope**:

1. Review the configured **Offer Name**, **Offer Description**, **Offer Duration**, and **Eligibility Level**.
2. Confirm the configured **Offer Applicability**—**Issuer**, **Program Type**, and **Program**.
3. Click **Continue** to proceed to the **Offer User Base** configuration.
