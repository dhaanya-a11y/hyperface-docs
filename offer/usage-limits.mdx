## What are Usage Limits?

Usage Limits cap how much benefit a single customer can earn from an offer over a given period — daily, weekly, monthly, or across the offer's entire lifetime.

## Why is it used?

It exists to prevent any one customer from earning an unbounded amount from a campaign and to help manage the campaign's overall cost exposure.
