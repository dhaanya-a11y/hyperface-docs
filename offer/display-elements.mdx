## What are Display Elements?

Display Elements control how an offer is presented to customers in the Hyperface Progressive Web Application (PWA) — covering both how it appears on the customer's home page and how its details are shown once opened.

## Why is it used?

It exists so that each offer has a clear, consistent, and engaging customer-facing presentation, independent of how the offer itself is configured behind the scenes.
