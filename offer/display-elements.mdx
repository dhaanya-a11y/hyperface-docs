## What are Display Elements?

The **Display Elements** section defines how an offer is presented to customers across the **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. These settings control the information displayed on the **Home Page**, **Offer Page**, and **Offer Details Page**, ensuring a consistent and engaging customer experience.

This configuration is common across **Transactional**, **Milestone**, **Merchant**, and **Gamified Offers**.

<Note>
Display Elements affect only the **customer-facing presentation** of an offer. They do not impact the offer's eligibility, reward calculation, posting, or budget configuration.
</Note>

## Home Page Configuration
Configure how the offer appears on the customer's **Home Page**.

The Home Page is typically the first place where customers discover available offers. Configure the display information carefully to make the offer easy to identify and understand.

### Home Page Fields

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Enable Display** | Enables or disables the visibility of the offer in the PWA. | Enabled |
| **Labels** | Assign one or more labels to categorize the offer. | Cashback, Fuel |
| **Banner Title** | Title displayed on the offer banner. | Fuel Cashback Week |
| **Banner Description** | Short description displayed on the banner. | Earn cashback on every fuel transaction. |
| **Display Title** | Primary title displayed to customers. | 5% Fuel Cashback |
| **Display Description** | Detailed description displayed in the PWA. | Earn 5% cashback on eligible fuel transactions throughout the campaign period. |
| **Display Order** | Determines the order in which the offer appears in the PWA. | 1 |
| **Display Color** | Primary theme color used for the offer card. | #0099FF |
| **Offer/Merchant Logo** | Upload the logo displayed with the offer. | fuel-logo.png |
| **Background Illustration** | Upload the background image displayed on the offer card. | fuel-banner.png |

<Tip>
Use concise titles, meaningful descriptions, and high-quality images to improve offer visibility and customer engagement.
</Tip>

The Offer Details Page provides customers with a complete understanding of the offer, including benefits, eligibility information, promotional content, and additional guidance.

### Section Details
Create one or more content sections to organize the information displayed on the Offer Details Page.
| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Section Title** | Title displayed for a content section. | Offer Benefits |
| **Section Content** | Rich text content describing the offer. | Get 5% cashback on eligible fuel transactions. |
| **Add Condition** | Adds another section to display additional information. | Terms & Conditions |
**Examples of additional sections include:**
- Offer Benefits
- Eligibility Criteria
- How to Redeem
- Terms & Conditions
- FAQs

---
## Call-to-Action (CTA)
Configure the button displayed on the **Offer Details Page**.
The CTA guides customers to perform an action, such as learning more about the offer or visiting an external website.
| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Text on Button** | Text displayed on the CTA button. | Know More |
| **Redirection Link** | URL opened when customers click the CTA button. | https://example.com/offers/fuel |
<Tip>
Use action-oriented CTA text such as **Know More**, **Learn More**, or **Visit Website** to clearly communicate the next action to customers.
</Tip>

## What Happens After Configuration?
Once the **Display Elements** are configured, the offer is presented consistently across the Progressive Web App.
Customers can view:
- The offer on the **Home Page**
- The offer summary on the **Offer Page**
- Complete offer information on the **Offer Details Page**
- Configured images, branding, and promotional content
- Call-to-Action buttons and redirection links

---

## Complete the Configuration
After configuring the **Display Elements**:
1. Verify the **Home Page** configuration.
2. Review the **Offer Page** information.
3. Confirm the **Offer Details Page** content.
4. Verify the **Call-to-Action (CTA)** configuration.
5. Review any **Advanced Settings**, if configured.
6. Click **Continue** to proceed to the **Communication** configuration.
